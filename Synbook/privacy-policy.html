  • Surname, first name
  • Address
  • E-mail address, telephone number
  • Age, sex
For the completion of your profile or within the scope of your account:
  • Photos and Videos
  • Information about your personality
  • Your interests and skills
  • Your profession
For an app-in purchase:
  • Information on the payment service provider
  • Debit or credit card number or other financial information

  • Any information you provide to us during the interaction. We may monitor or log this communication for training purposes and to ensure a high quality of service.

4.   What personal data do we collect from you when you use the app?

We collect the following personal data from your mobile phone when you use the app:

  • IP address, device ID and type, device-specific and application-specific settings and features, app crashes
  • Advertising IDs (such as Google's AAID and Apple's IDFA, both randomly generated numbers that you can reset in the device settings),
  • Browser type, version and language, operating system, time zones, technical identifiers associated with cookies or other technologies that uniquely identify your device or browser (e.g., IMEI/UDID and MAC address);
  • Service Provider and Signal Strength
  • Date and time of registration
  • Functions used
  • App-in purchases made
  • Advertisement you click on
  • Interaction with another user (e.g. time and date of your exchange, number of messages)
If you explicitly agree to the fact that each time you restart the app, we may collect the following information about you and provide the service in the first place:
  • Geolocation data, i.e. the location where you are located (latitude and longitude).

  • Fulfilment of the contract or is necessary for pre-contractual measures: The processing of personal data is carried out at your request for the purposes stated, but under certain circumstances the scope of services of our app may be restricted or our services may be subject to a charge.

You may object to this processing (see para.12).

In the latter case, you have a general right of objection, which is implemented by us without stating a particular situation.

15.   Data Security

We have technical and organizational security procedures in place to safeguard the security of your personal data and to protect your session data and personal data against unauthorised or unlawful processing and/or against accidental loss, alteration, disclosure or access.

However, you should always be aware that the transmission of information via the Internet and other electronic means involves certain security risks and that we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted in this way.

Your personal information in connection with our website will be transmitted SSL-encrypted from the app to us. We take technical and organizational measures to protect our app from unauthorized access.

If you notice any incidents in connection with our website that appear unusual to you, we would be very grateful if you would notify us immediately.

16.   Storage Period

We keep your data for the following period:

  • Data that we process by law is retained for the statutory period of retention.
  • Data that we need to fulfil a contract will be retained for at least the duration of the contract and for a maximum of 10 years thereafter, unless we need the data to assert our rights.
  • Data that we process to protect our legitimate interests will be stored for a maximum of ten years after the end of the contractual relationship, unless we need the data to assert our rights.
  • Data that we no longer need will be deleted or destroyed if the purpose of the processing and the processing basis become invalid, unless there is a legal obligation to store.
